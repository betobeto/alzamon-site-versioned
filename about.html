About The Author

Thank you for stopping by. My name is Alberto González, a Costa Rica-based illustrator and graphics artist with a degree from the University of Costa Rica in graphic design. In fact this site has been entirely built in code by yours truly, having complete control. Here I am focusing on my Children's Illustration and Character Design portfolio to seek professional opportunities in this area.

I am currently looking for agent representation in illustration affairs, specially children's-oriented illustration, animation, comic books and/or graphic novels. I provide more than 20 years of professional experience and responsibility to take on any assignment.
