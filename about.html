About Me

The requisite ego-stroking section. I'll keep this short.

I am Alberto González, a Costa Rican born illustrator, comics artist, character designer, 3D modeler and UX developer holding a BFA from the University of Costa Rica in graphic arts and design.

Alzamon is the artistic pseudonym I've adopted, composed from fragments of my full name. (I tell here some more about it). My decades-long experience on web design and development allows me to build my portfolio websites in customized form, something I feel important for my own image.

In addition to providing creative services to the creative community, I am currently looking for agent representation in illustration affairs, specially children's-oriented illustration, animation, comic books and/or graphic novels.

I am also fluent in English, Spanish, French and Portuguese languages. As of mid-2023, I'm beginning studies on Japanese. For me, languages are the key to understand and throughly know cultures across the world.
